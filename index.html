// 01 — About

The person
behind the code.

I'm Esdras Buelvas — a developer, writer, and creative builder based in the US. My work lives at the crossroads of technology and human experience.

I started coding because I wanted to build things that actually help people — tools, experiences, stories. Even if most of my work is theoretical, I will put my name out there. That drive hasn't changed. Whether it's a slick web app, an AI experiment, or a long-form essay, I approach every project with the same obsessive attention to craft.

I'm currently exploring the edges of AI-assisted creative work, building personal tools, and developing my fiction project — The Unwritten Book of Bushido.

When I'm not building, I'm writing, reading philosophy, or thinking about systems — human and digital.
